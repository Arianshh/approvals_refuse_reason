Approval Refuse Reason

Enhance Your Odoo Approval Workflows with Refuse Reason Tracking

Empower approvers to provide reasons for request refusal, improving transparency and communication.

Key Features:

  • Wizard for capturing reasons when an approver refuses a request.
  • Refuse reasons are stored and visible in the approvers' notebook for each request.
  • Enhanced transparency and communication in the approval process.

How It Works:

The "Approval Refuse Reason" module extends the functionality of the Odoo approval app. When an approver decides to refuse a request, a wizard pops up, prompting the approver to provide a reason for the refusal.

These refuse reasons are stored and can be viewed in the approvers' notebook for each request. This feature enhances transparency and communication in the approval process, allowing requesters to understand why their request was refused.
